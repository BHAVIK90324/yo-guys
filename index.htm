Did you know that the tradition of hanging stockings by the fireplace comes from the legend of St. Nicholas? According to the story, St. Nicholas wanted to help a poor man and his three daughters. He heard that the man couldn't afford dowries for his daughters, which meant they might not be able to marry. So, St. Nicholas decided to help by tossing three bags of gold coins down the chimney, and they landed in the stockings the girls had hung by the fireplace to dry. This is said to be the origin of the Christmas stocking tradition!
